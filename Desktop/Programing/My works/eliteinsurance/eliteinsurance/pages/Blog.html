Search For Care The Way You Search For
Everything Else: On Your Phone

An excellent grasp of the claims system and overall workflow.
Developed a comprehensive understanding of the claims review processes that currently exist and additional processes that are needed to improve consistency and quality of review. Become well-acquainted with internal tools and technical processes.
Worked to drive down the pending queue of claims issues.
Prepared and presented status of pending claims issues and developed initial action plans to reduce. Begun to form strong working relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
Become an inhouse expert on all claims related matters to answer questions on the adjudication process and individual claims.
Support the claims team by identifying issues and presenting resolutions.
Take ownership of all claims processing issues; monitoring, tracking, and statusing the health of the operation.
Manage the escalation and resolution process with external vendors to ensure appropriate turnaround times.
Contribute to team wide goals to improve claims processes and integrate additional functions into our daily operations.
