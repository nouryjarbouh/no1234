Claims Examiner

Customer Service

Role description

In this role, you will process and investigate our clients’ claims. You will ensure timely and accurate adjudication of claims, uncover process discrepancies, and answer escalated customer and provider questions. Additionally, we will be looking to you to help us build our claims examination processes.

Qualifications

A curious mind and attention to detail. Willingness to grow, learn and work as a member of a cross-functional team. A minimum of 5 years experience examining medical claims with an insurance carrier or third party administrator. Thorough knowledge of of adjustment processing, medical terminology, CPT, and revenue codes. Strong quantitative analysis skills. Strong Excel and initial SQL skills (or the ability to pick it up quickly). Strong verbal and written communications, and interpersonal interactions (e.g. partnering, conflict management, consulting, etc.). Ability to work independently and drive projects to completion. A bachelor’s degree In depth knowledge of claims processing protocols and payment schemes. Experience handling Personal Health Information (PHI) in a professional manner. Experience manipulating and entering accurate data in a production driven environment. A relevant professional designation or certification (AIC, ICD 9/10, etc) Demonstrated capacity to balance competing priorities and continue to execute projects with excellence.

Position Details

An excellent grasp of the claims system and overall workflow. Developed a comprehensive understanding of the claims review processes that currently exist and additional processes that are needed to improve consistency and quality of review. Become well-acquainted with internal tools and technical processes. Worked to drive down the pending queue of claims issues. Prepared and presented status of pending claims issues and developed initial action plans to reduce. Begun to form strong working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. Become an inhouse expert on all claims related matters to answer questions on the adjudication process and individual claims. Support the claims team by identifying issues and presenting resolutions. Take ownership of all claims processing issues; monitoring, tracking, and statusing the health of the operation. Manage the escalation and resolution process with external vendors to ensure appropriate turnaround times. Contribute to team wide goals to improve claims processes and integrate additional functions into our daily operations.
