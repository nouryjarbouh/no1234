Basic Minimum Coverage

We’ll make sure you meet the minimum cover as set by UAE law, which must include general practitioner visits, emergency treatments, referrals to specialists as well as surgical, investigative and maternity procedures. We’ll also guide you with any additional options you may like to include.

Healthcare Network

Select from a range of reputable healthcare networks, with access to great hospitals and doctors to make sure your employees have access to quality care. Select from local, regional or international healthcare plans approved by UAE health regulators based on where you’d like to be covered.

Types of Plans

All the information you need to select from two types of treatment. The in-patient only plan is a cost effective solution that only covers the financial expenses that occur during hospitalization. Outpatient treatment involves cover of treatment by a physician without in-hospital admission.
