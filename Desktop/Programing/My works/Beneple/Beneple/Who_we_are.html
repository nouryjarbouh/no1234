People+Benefit=Beneple

We are business owners, just like you

Beneple was conceived by a group of entrepreneurs from diverse backgrounds based in the UAE, who were tired of wasting time and money on laborious and mundane HR tasks.


Who wants to deal with things like public liability insurance and visa applications when there is so much work to be done to develop and expand your business?!


Dealing with real life challenges, experienced while setting up our own companies, we realized there was a gap in the market for something truly life changing in the SME world: a simplified and automated system that handles all the routine HR tasks we hate.


Don't waste your capital on large elaborate systems designed for large corporations. Our integrated HR online software platform is tailored for the UAE market and is guaranteed to simplify your life as much as it has ours.


Confused about your company's health and medical insurance? If you already have an existing HR system that you're happy with but would like our help obtaining the best insurance quotes for your business and employees, we can also help you with this, regardless of whether or not you're using the Beneple system.
