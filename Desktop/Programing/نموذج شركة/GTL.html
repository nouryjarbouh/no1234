GTL

GTL is focused on building cloud software that introduce a whole new agility to online businesses.

Currently GTL software is available to the clients of its product agency Mokus.

GTL uses: Scala, a mix of database engines (graph, SQL and document based), spark, custom designed statistical models, a state of the art micro services architecture, and a bunch of machine learning algorithms.

GTL's approach is an integrated platform that manages customer primary data, product development tech, hardware and secondary data together which enables the software to be intelligent enough to implement product optimisations at unmatched speed.
